Let me introduce myself…

Hi, I'm Steph! I'm a twenty-four year old activities co-ordinator from Essex and mother to a beautiful little girl. In my spare time I train as an Aerialist in disciplines such as Trapeze, Aerial Hoop and Aerial Silks. I also teach Hula Hoop dance to a lovely bunch of students. I enjoys listening to a wide range of music, web developing and design, sketching, wildlife and spending time with my family.
