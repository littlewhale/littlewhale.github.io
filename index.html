Introduction

A dedicated and determined learner

In my spare time I train as an Aerialist in disciplines such as Trapeze, Aerial Hoop and Aerial Silks, I also teach Hula Hoop dance to a lovely bunch of students. I enjoys listening to a wide range of music, web developing and design, sketching, spending time with my family and wildlife.
